Frequently Asked Beauty salon Questions

+ “WHAT BRAND OF MAKEUP DO YOU USE?

While the wedding planner is setting up and the caterer is in the reception hall, your makeup artist and hair stylist are right by your side as you get ready to walk down the aisle. Asking for a phone consultation is a good way to get a feel of who your stylist is before hiring them. If you can’t enjoy conversations with your stylist over the phone, you definitely don’t want them in the same room as you on your Big Day.

+ “WHAT BRAND OF MAKEUP DO YOU USE? IS IT HYPOALLERGENIC?”

If anyone in your bridal party has allergies, it’s important to know if your makeup artist’s products are hypoallergenic. The last thing any bride or bridesmaid wants on the Big Day is to have a bad reaction to her foundation!

+ “ARE YOU COMFORTABLE WITH DIFFERENT ETHNICITIES?”

There are dozens of different skin shades and hair textures, and not every makeup artist or hair stylist has proficient in them all! If you have a diverse bridal party, you need an artist and a stylist who can make you and every one of your bridesmaids feel confident and beautiful.

+ “ARE YOU COMFORTABLE WITH MATURE SKIN?”

On a similar note, many makeup artists are only accustomed to young brides and bridesmaids. Not every makeup artist is prepared to make-over your mother- or grandmother-in-law. If a member of your bridal party or one of the family has mature skin, make sure to ask your potential makeup artist if they’re prepared to accommodate that person.

+ “IS A TRIAL INCLUDED IN THE BRIDAL PACKAGE?”

A trial run is always a good idea. Make sure you know if you’ll be paying extra for one!
